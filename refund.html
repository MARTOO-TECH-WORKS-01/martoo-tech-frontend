Refund Policy

Our policy regarding refunds for courses and services

Last updated: March 2026

Course Refunds

If you are not satisfied with a course, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase, provided you have completed less than 30% of the course content. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Service Refunds

For services rendered (e.g., data analysis, software installation), refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us within 48 hours if you are unsatisfied.

Software Downloads

Software downloads are non-refundable due to the nature of digital products. However, if you encounter technical issues, we will assist in resolving them.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at martootechworks@gmail.com with your order details and reason for refund. We will respond within 2 business days.

Exceptions

No refunds will be issued for violations of our Terms of Service or for services already fully rendered.
